Essential Breathing is a simple visual breathing app for Android. A ball moves upward during inhalation and downward during exhalation, helping you follow a steady breathing rhythm without distractions.

Features:

* visual breathing guide with a vertical gauge
* adjustable inhale and exhale durations
* adjustable session duration
* start, pause, resume, and stop controls
* several calm visual themes
* English, French, and Spanish interface
* no account, no ads, no tracking

The app is intentionally minimal: choose a rhythm, press play, and breathe.
